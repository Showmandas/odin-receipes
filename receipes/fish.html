Baja Style Fish Tacos

I came up with this recipe after having fish tacos from a street vendor in Ensenada, Mexico. It is the closest to authentic I have found. Salsa can be made a day in advance for flavors to blend.

Ingredients

  • 6 tomatoes, diced
  • ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • ½ teaspoon garlic salt
  • ½ lime, juiced

Steps

  1. Toss coleslaw mix with ranch dressing in a bowl; set aside for flavors to blend.
  2. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. Stir batter mix and beer together in a bowl. Wrap corn tortillas in wet paper towels and set aside.
  4. Microwave corn tortillas on high until warmed, about 1 minute.
